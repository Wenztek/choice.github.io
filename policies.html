Policies and Tenant Obligations

Stalls Rentals

  • Rent is due on the 15th of the month.
  • 6 month or yearly contracts are available. Monthly rates are available at a premium. No refunds.
  • $25 late charge is applied to accounts and access is denied if payments are not made on time.
  • Partial payments do not eliminate late fees. Payments must be on time and in full.
  • Minimum of 2 weeks’ notice is required for move outs.
  • NSF charge is $45.00 per incident. Alternative methods of payment may be required.

Storage Unit Rentals

  • Rent is due on the 1st of the month.
  • Damage deposits are refunded if storage unit is:
    1. Completely empty.
    2. Swept clean.
    3. No stains or spills left behind and
    4. Proper notice was received.
  • $25 late charge is applied to accounts and access is denied if payments are not made on time.  Units are double locked when rent is overdue.
  • Partial payments do not eliminate late fees. Payments must be on time and in full.
  • Minimum of 2 weeks’ notice is required for move outs.
  • NSF charge is $45.00 per incident. Alternative methods of payment may be required.
  • No combustibles, radioactive, corrosive goods or food items to be stored.
  • Not to use the premises for the operation of any business.
  • Not to assign or sublet the premises.
  • Not to use the premises for any illegal premises.
  • Door pull handles and rope must be left INSIDE the door.

Policies

  • No backing up to storage unit doors. Load / unload parallel to the buildings only!
  • Do not leave any trash behind. All trash must be taken with you.
  • Please back into stalls, centre with marker and leave 2-3 feet behind for weed control.
  • One vehicle per stall/unit.
  • Nothing is to be left on the ground at any time. (unused blocking will be discarded)
  • All steps, awnings & bump outs must be left in the travel position.
  • No flushing plumbing in CSS yard.
  • Vehicle repairs not permitted in CSS yard.
  • Any tarps in use must be fastened down. Tarps left loose will be removed and discarded.  Be aware that our weed control program may be harmful to pets and children.

DO NOT BLOCK GATE ACCESS. Please park in the designated areas only.

*Policies & Obligations are subject to change without notice.
