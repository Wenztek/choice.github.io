About Us

Choice Storage Solutions is locally owned. Our vision is providing customers with secure, accessible indoor, and RV Storage, while continuing to offer fair rates.

Choice Storage Solutions continues to make improvements to the facility in an effort to offer the best possible solutions to your storage needs.

  • 24 Hour Access
  • Safe easy access via Card Pass or Key Fob
  • Paved Access to the Site
  • On-site Cameras
  • Fenced Compound - 8' Concrete Wall (East side), Commercial Grade Chain Link (West Side)
  • Modern Mini Storage Buildings
  • Multiple Choices of Indoor Storage including Sea Cans
  • RV Parking for all types including Boats
  • Gravelled and Groomed Parking Stalls
  • Drainage System
  • Weed Control Program
  • Rodent Control
